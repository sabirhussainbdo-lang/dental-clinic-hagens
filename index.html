Our Story

A Family Legacy of
Excellence

Dental Clinic Hagens is a family business founded by Dr. Harold Hagens in 1982 on Aruba after graduating from Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. His daughter, Dr. Mandy Hagens, expanded the practice to Amstelveen, Netherlands — bringing the same values of quality and personal care.

We treat patients of all ages with the newest techniques and the finest materials, ensuring you always receive the highest standard of care in a warm, welcoming setting.

  • ✓ Latest dental technology & materials
  • ✓ Extra attention for anxious patients & children
  • ✓ Transparent pricing with clear communication
  • ✓ Family-run practice since 1982 — two continents
  • ✓ Free parking available at our Amstelveen clinic
